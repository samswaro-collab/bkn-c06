🔥 HOT — £686M contract Apr 2026
📍 Warton, Lancashire UK · Edgewing JV (BAE 33.3% + Leonardo + JAIEC) · Combat Air Flying Demonstrator unveiled Jul 2025
The Global Combat Air Programme (GCAP) is the UK-Italy-Japan 6th-generation fighter. Edgewing JV was formed June 2025 and immediately awarded a £686M joint international development contract in April 2026. The flying demonstrator design was unveiled at RIAT July 2025. BAE leads design and systems integration from Warton. This program will ramp engineering teams significantly through the 2020s toward a 2035 in-service target — with initial NPI engineering headcount builds happening now.
Cyient angle: MBSE/SysML for systems architecture, CAD/CAM (CATIA) structural design, avionics integration support, digital engineering (PLM/Teamcenter), simulation & modelling

🚢 Type 26 Frigate Production Ramp — Govan, Scotland
🔥 HOT — Norway £10B deal + HMS Glasgow 2026
